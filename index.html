Information About me

I spent a large part of my childhood in Karachi, Pakistan and currently reside in Montreal, Canada. Ever since I was a child, I've always been fond of videogames. As such, after graduating from highschool I knew I wanted to be able to create my own, leading me to pursue a degree in Game Development from Concordia University in Montreal.

I enjoyed my time so much that I decided this was the career I wanted to pursue, taking a game development internship in 2021 during the pandemic. My goal is to create games that are able to resonate with players, offering a fun escape from their day to day lives. As far as I can see, making fun and immersive games is the path I see myself following.
